Getting Started

How do I check if fiber is available in my area?

+

Simply go to our Check Availability page, enter your address and pincode, and we'll instantly show you if fiber is available at your location.

What's the installation process?

+

1. Place your order through our website 2. Choose your installation date 3. Our technician visits your location 4. We set up your connection 5. Speed test & activation. The whole process takes 24-48 hours.

What do I need for installation?

+

You'll need: 1. Accessible fiber line point 2. Power outlet near installation point 3. Basic interior routing (optional - technician can help) 4. Valid ID for verification 5. Contact phone number

Billing & Plans

When will I be billed?

+

Billing starts from your installation date on a monthly cycle. First month is prorated if installation happens mid-month. You'll receive an invoice via email before each billing date.

Can I change my plan?

+

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll adjust your billing accordingly. No fees or penalties for plan changes.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept: Credit/Debit cards, Net banking, UPI, NEFT/RTGS, and Standing instructions for auto-payment. Choose what works best for you at checkout.

Troubleshooting

I'm getting low speeds. What can I do?

+

First, try restarting your router. Check if other devices are using bandwidth. Test directly with ethernet cable. If speeds are still low, we guarantee your subscribed speed or we'll credit your account.

My connection is down. What do I do?

+

1. Check if the router is powered on 2. Restart your router 3. Check cables are connected 4. Call us at +91 98765 43210 5. We'll troubleshoot or send a technician. Expected resolution: 1-4 hours.

Why is my router showing red/orange light?

+

Red/Orange light indicates connection issue. Try: 1. Power cycle the router 2. Check cable connections 3. Move closer to router 4. Check for interference from microwaves 5. Contact support if issue persists.

Policies

What's your cancellation policy?

+

You can cancel anytime with no penalties. We'll accept cancellation requests via phone, email, or our portal. Final bill will be prepared based on usage till cancellation date.

Do I have a data cap?

+

No! All FiberX plans include unlimited data. No data caps, no throttling, no hidden limits. Use as much as you want at your subscribed speed.

What if I move to a new location?

+

We can help you transfer your service if fiber is available at your new address. There's no transfer fee. Contact us at least 7 days before your move to arrange this.
